Sorter for Nikke characters. Pick your filters if desired, then hit the Start button.

Make sure to hit the two "Exclude alts" filters if you are doing a pure character ranking (versus gameplay rank).

Click on the character you like better from the two, or tie them if you like them equally or don't know them.


KB controls: Pick Left = H. Pick Right = L. Undo = J. Tie = K. Save = S.

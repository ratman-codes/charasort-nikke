Sorter for Nikke characters. Pick your filters if desired, then hit the Start button.

Click on the Nikke you like better from the two, or hit tie if you like them equally or don't know them.

Filters alts (SSR and SR) by default for character/popularity ranking. Untick these for a gaming ranking.


KB controls: Pick Left = H. Pick Right = L. Undo = J. Tie = K. Save = S.
